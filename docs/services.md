## Our Six Service Lines

### 1. Train (Free)

The complete AI bootcamp -- 12 phases, 35 lessons, 15 real projects. Learn everything from Python basics to deploying AI agents. Runs 100% locally on your machine with a Socratic AI tutor.

**Cost:** Free, forever. [Get Started](getting-started.md)

### 2. Assess

Technical assessments designed to evaluate real-world AI development skills. Not multiple choice -- you write code, build solutions, and solve problems. Graded by automated tests and AI rubric review.

### 3. Proctor

Assessments are taken in a proctored environment:
- **Timed** -- complete within the allotted time
- **Locked** -- no access to solutions or external help during the exam
- **Monitored** -- tab switches and focus changes are logged
- **Certified** -- passing earns a Grey8 certificate with a publicly verifiable credential

Your certificate includes a verification code (e.g., `G8-A3F7K9`) that anyone -- including employers -- can verify online.

### 4. Place

Certified candidates become eligible for placement with our employer partners. Grey8 matches your skills with open roles:
- Contract or full-time positions
- AI/ML engineering, data science, backend development
- Your skills matrix and portfolio are visible to employers on the platform

### 5. Bootcamp (Enterprise)

Cohort-based AI training for enterprise teams:
- Custom curriculum tracks based on team needs
- Dedicated assessments and progress tracking
- Training manager dashboard for cohort oversight
- Ideal for upskilling existing teams in AI development

### 6. Consulting

Remote AI consulting engagements staffed by Grey8-certified developers:
- Project-based or ongoing retainer
- Grey8 manages the engagement, you get vetted talent
- Full-stack AI development: RAG systems, agents, APIs, deployment

## For Candidates Seeking Certification

Completed the bootcamp? Here's the certification path:

1. **Sign up** on the Grey8 Enterprise portal
2. **Take the assessment** -- a proctored technical exam covering the skills you learned
3. **Get graded** -- AI + manual review of your submissions
4. **Earn your certificate** -- a publicly verifiable credential with your skills and score
5. **Get discovered** -- employers can find you through the Grey8 placement portal

**What employers see:** Your skills matrix, composite score, portfolio of completed projects, and verification link.
