## How to Sign

When you open your first pull request, an automated check will ask you to sign
this CLA. To sign, add a **comment on your pull request** with exactly this text:

```
I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA
```

Your GitHub username and the signing date are recorded in
[`signatures/version1/cla.json`](signatures/version1/cla.json). You sign only
once — future pull requests are recognized automatically.

If you contribute on behalf of an employer or other legal entity, ensure you are
authorized to do so (see Sections 4 and 12) before signing.
